06 Days Skardu & Deosai

Pick up Cities : LHR – ISB – FSD

DAY 00

  • 09:00 PM – Received participants from Lahore
  • Short stop at Bhera Rest Area
  • Traveling to Islamabad

DAY 01

  • 2:30 AM – Reach Rawalpindi
  • Pick participants from 26 No. Daewoo Terminal
  • 3:30 AM – Traveling to Chilas
  • Via Hazara Motorway & KKH
  • Breakfast at Besham
  • Sightseeing: Dam Site, Jijal, River Indus, Summer Nala
  • Reach Chilas till evening – Night stay in Chilas

DAY 02

  • 8:00 AM – Breakfast
  • 9:00 AM – Traveling to Skardu Valley
  • Visit Nanga Parbat View Point
  • Visit Junction of Mountain Ranges
  • Traveling via adventurous Skardu Road
  • Visit Upper Kachura Lake & Shangrila Resort
  • Till night reach Skardu – Night stay

DAY 03

  • Breakfast
  • Travel to Manthokha Waterfall
  • Visit Kharmang Valley
  • Visit Shigar Valley
  • Visit Sarfaranga Cold Desert
  • Visit Shigar Fort & travel back to Skardu
  • Night stay in Skardu

DAY 04

  • Early morning
  • Jeep safari towards Deosai – Full day
  • Explore Deosai: Sadpara Lake, Bara Pani & more
  • Jeep ride towards Basho Valley
  • Explore and return till evening

DAY 05

  • Visit any remaining place in Skardu
  • Travel back to Chilas – sightseeing on the way
  • Short stop at Astore / Astak Nala
  • Visit River Indus, Roundu, Haramosh
  • Stay night in Chilas
  • Final night of tour – Dinner in Chilas

DAY 06

  • Early morning travel back to Lahore
  • Breakfast on the way
  • 6:00 PM – Reach Islamabad
  • 12:00 AM – Reach Lahore
