08 Days Skardu

Pick up Cities : LHR – ISB – FSD

DAY 00

  • 09:00 PM – Received participants from Lahore
  • Short stop at Bhera Rest Area
  • Traveling to Mansehra

DAY 01

  • Arrival at Mansehra
  • Breakfast at Balakot / Kiwai
  • Travel towards Naran
  • Sightseeing and glaciers on the way to Naran
  • Reach Naran Bazaar
  • Dinner & night stay in Naran / Barawai

DAY 02

  • Breakfast at 08:00 AM
  • Visit Lulusar Lake
  • Short stay at Babusar Top
  • Visit Chilas Zero Point, Tatta Pani & Nanga Parbat View Points
  • Mountain Ranges Junction Point
  • Rakaposhi View Point sightseeing
  • Travel to Skardu Valley via adventurous Skardu Road
  • Visit Shangrila Resort & Upper Kachura Lake
  • Night stay in Skardu Hotel

DAY 03

  • Breakfast at 07:00 AM
  • Travel to Shigar Valley
  • Sarfaranga Cold Desert
  • Jeep & Air Safari at Sarfaranga Cold Desert
  • Visit Shigar Fort
  • Travel to Manthoka Waterfall – sightseeing
  • Back to Skardu – Dinner & night stay

DAY 04

  • Breakfast at Hotel
  • Jeep Safari to Deosai Plains
  • Visit Sadpara Lake
  • Visit Malik Top
  • Explore Deosai National Park
  • Visit Kala Pani, Bara Pani, Sheosar Lake
  • Return to Skardu – Overnight stay

DAY 05

  • Breakfast at 08:00 AM
  • Traveling towards Hunza – sightseeing on the way
  • Haramosh Gilgit
  • Reach Nomal Jeep Point – transfer to 4×4 Jeeps for Naltar Valley
  • Enjoy Naltar Valley
  • Rakaposhi View Point
  • Dinner & night stay in Hunza

DAY 06

  • Breakfast at Hotel
  • Traveling towards China Border
  • Visit Attabad Lake – Boating & Jet Ski
  • Visit Hussaini Bridge & Passu Cones
  • Visit Sost Bazar & China Border
  • Explore Khunjerab Pass
  • Dinner & night stay in Hunza

DAY 07

  • Breakfast at Hotel
  • Visit Altit Fort & Baltit Fort in Hunza
  • Travel towards Gilgit – Mountain junction sightseeing
  • Short stop at Babusar Top
  • Dinner & night stay in Naran / Barawai

DAY 08

  • Breakfast at Hotel
  • Traveling to Islamabad
  • Optional: Saif-ul-Malook Lake visit
  • Stop for refreshments at Kiwai / Balakot
  • Visit Khanpur Dam – Boating & Parasailing
  • Reach Islamabad by 7:00 PM – End of services
