05 Days Fairy Meadows

Pick up Cities : LHR – ISB – FSD

DAY 00

  • 10:00 PM – Received participants from Lahore
  • Short stop at Bhera Rest Area
  • Traveling to Islamabad

DAY 01

  • Pick participants from Islamabad
  • Travel towards Hunza via Naran & Babusar Top
  • Arrival at Balakot
  • Breakfast at Balakot
  • Travel towards Naran
  • Visit Batakundi
  • Visit Lulu SAR Lake
  • Dinner & night stay in Barawai

DAY 02

  • Breakfast at 8:00 AM
  • Visit Babusar Top – Enjoy your time there
  • Departure for Raikot at 9:00 AM
  • Arrival at Raikot & transfer to jeeps
  • Start hike towards Fairy Meadows by afternoon
  • Arrival in Fairy Meadows – transfer to camps/huts
  • Dinner & bonfire at 8:00 PM
  • Overnight stay in camps/hotel

DAY 03

  • Breakfast at 7:00 AM
  • Start hike towards Beyal Camp
  • Explore Beyal Camp
  • Reach Base Camp Nanga Parbat
  • Trace back to Fairy Meadows (reach by 6:00 PM)
  • Dinner & bonfire at 8:00 PM
  • Overnight stay in camps/hotel

DAY 04

  • Breakfast at 8:00 AM
  • Trek down to Tatu & transfer to jeeps at 11:00 AM
  • Departure for Raikot at 12:00 PM
  • Arrival at Raikot & departure for Chilas
  • Arrival at Chilas – Dinner at 8:00 PM
  • Overnight stay in Barawai / Naran

DAY 05

  • Breakfast at hotel
  • Traveling back to Islamabad (sightseeing on the way)
  • Lunch break
  • Reach Islamabad by 8:00 PM
  • Drop Islamabad participants
  • Back to Lahore around 12:00 AM (+/- 2 hours)
  • Meals: Breakfast + Lunch
