DAY 01

  • Pick Participants from Islamabad.
  • Sightseeing at Murree Expressway.
  • Straight towards Muzaffarabad.
  • Breakfast at Muzaffarabad.
  • Visit Dhani Waterfall.
  • Short stay at LOC.
  • Visit Kutton Waterfall.
  • Dinner & Night Stay at Keran/Sharda.

DAY 02

  • Breakfast at Hotel.
  • Shift into Jeeps.
  • Move towards Kel.
  • Reach Kel.
  • Get Doli for Arang Kel.
  • 30–45 min hike.
  • Photo session at Arang Kel.
  • Witness the beauty of Arang Kel.
  • Leave for Taobat.
  • Dinner & Night Stay at Taobat.

DAY 03

  • Breakfast at Hotel.
  • Explore Taobat Village.
  • Visit Hemlet.
  • Leave back to Sharda.
  • BBQ Night at Hotel.
  • Enjoy Bonfire.
  • Dinner & Night Stay at Sharda/Keran.

DAY 04

  • Breakfast (early morning).
  • Departure back to Lahore.
  • Short Stop Enroute.
  • Islamabad stopovers.
  • Reach back home.
  • End of services.
