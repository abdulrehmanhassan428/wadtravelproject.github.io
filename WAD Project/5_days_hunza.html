05 Days Hunza Valley

Pick up Cities : LHR – ISB – FSD

DAY 00

  • 10:00 PM – Received participants from Lahore
  • Short stop at Bhera Rest Area
  • Traveling to Islamabad

DAY 01

  • Pick participants from Islamabad
  • Travel towards Hunza via Naran & Babusar Top
  • Arrival at Balakot
  • Breakfast at Balakot
  • Leave for Hunza
  • Visit Kiwai Waterfall on the way
  • Short stop at Naran Bazaar
  • Move towards Babusar Top
  • Photography & short stay at Babusar Top
  • Dinner at Barawai / Batakundi / Chilas
  • Night stay at Barawai / Batakundi / Chilas
  • Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

DAY 02

  • Breakfast at 7:30 AM
  • Departure for Hunza Valley
  • Visit Nanga Parbat View Point
  • Visit Mountain Ranges Junction
  • Photography & short stay at Junction Point
  • Visit Rakaposhi View Point
  • Visit Altit Fort & Baltit Fort
  • Explore Karimabad Bazaar
  • Dinner & night stay in Hunza
  • Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

DAY 03

  • Breakfast at Hunza
  • Traveling towards Attabad Lake via tunnels
  • Quality time & sightseeing at Attabad Lake
  • Hiking to Hussaini Suspension Bridge
  • Visit Passu Glacier & Passu Cones
  • Visit Khunjerab Pass
  • Back to Karimabad cultural bazaar
  • Enjoy BBQ, Bonfire / Musical Night
  • Dinner & night stay in Hunza
  • Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

DAY 04

  • Breakfast at 7:30 AM
  • Traveling towards Nomal Jeep Stop
  • Transfer to jeeps for Naltar Valley (charges equally divided)
  • Visit Ski Slope
  • Visit Ice Hockey Ground
  • Photography & short stay
  • Enjoy snow in Naltar Valley
  • Back to Jeep Stop
  • Travel back to Chilas / Barawai / Batakundi
  • Dinner & night stay
  • Meals: Breakfast & Dinner

DAY 05

  • Breakfast at 7:30 AM
  • Traveling back to Islamabad (Sightseeing on the way)
  • Lunch break
  • Reach Islamabad till 7 PM
  • Drop Islamabad participants
  • Back to Lahore around 12 AM (+/- 2 hours)
  • Meals: Breakfast + Lunch
